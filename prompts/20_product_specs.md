# prompts/20_product_pages.md

## Objective
Generate product page content for each Excel product with:
- Clear value proposition
- What’s included (Excel + short video guide)
- Who it’s for
- Key features (bullets)
- Screenshots placeholders (no fake images)
- CTA to buy
All in Spanish.

## Product list (Spanish names)
- Comparador de créditos hipotecarios
- Bola de Nieve - Elimina tus deudas
- Amortizador de deudas PRO
- Analizador de puntaje crediticio
- Presupuesto 50/30/20 (gratis)
- Calculadora de costos indirectos
- Gestor de costo de ventas
- ARPU Calculadora
- Dual Cashflow (Bs/USD)
- Calculadora de precio de venta
- Registro de ventas con código único
- Full Financial Statements

## Deliverables per product (Spanish)
- H1 title
- 2-line summary
- “Ideal para” (bullets)
- “Qué resuelve” (bullets)
- “Incluye” (Excel + video)
- “Cómo se usa” (3–5 steps)
- “Requisitos” (Excel version, etc.)
- FAQ (2–3 Qs)
- CTA button label

## Constraints
- No hype or unrealistic promises
- Tie outcomes to actions (use the tool correctly)
- Mention “Mis compras” access and 48h expiry link (when relevant)
