# prompts/10_copy_brand.md

## Objective
Generate landing and marketplace copy that:
- Increases YouTube subscriptions
- Converts visitors into buyers
- Feels friendly, direct, and anti-hype
- Uses Spanish for user-facing copy

## Rules
- All output copy must be in Spanish (because users see it).
- Avoid exaggerated claims. Use experience-based authority.
- Keep CTA labels short and clear.

## Deliverables
1) Landing hero:
- Main headline (Spanish)
- Subheadline (Spanish)
- Primary CTA (subscribe)
- Secondary CTA (marketplace)
- Micro-trust line (experience)

2) Product section copy:
- “Top products” short intro
- 1-liner value props per anchor product:
  - Amortizador de deudas PRO
  - Dual Cashflow (Bs/USD)
  - Full Financial Statements
  - Registro de ventas con código único

3) Lead magnet section:
- Copy for “Presupuesto 50/30/20 (gratis)”
- Email capture text
- Privacy reassurance line

4) How-it-works section (Spanish):
- Step 1: Elige tu plantilla
- Step 2: Paga por QR
- Step 3: Sube comprobante y descarga

5) FAQ (Spanish):
- Payment and proof upload
- Approval time expectations
- Download link expiry (48h)
- Access via “Mis compras”

## Paste-ready instruction
When generating copy, include:
- Section title
- Body copy
- Button labels
- Optional microcopy
