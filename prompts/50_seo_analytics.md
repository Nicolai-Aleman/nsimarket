# prompts/50_analytics_seo.md

## Objective
Design a minimal analytics + SEO plan that supports:
- Organic traffic growth
- Lead magnet conversions
- Product page indexing
- B2B lead capture opportunities

## Constraints
- MVP should avoid heavy paid tooling.
- Prefer lightweight analytics (privacy-friendly).
- Use SEO-friendly content structure.

## Deliverables
1) SEO structure:
- Marketplace index page
- Product pages with schema suggestions (no overkill)
- Blog/learn section suggestion (Phase 2)

2) Events to track (minimal):
- Subscribe CTA click
- Lead magnet download
- Checkout started
- Proof uploaded
- Order approved
- Download clicked

3) B2B lead capture:
- Simple form module suggestion (Phase 2)
- “Book a call” CTA placement suggestion

## Output rules
- Explain in English
- Any user-facing labels must be in Spanish
